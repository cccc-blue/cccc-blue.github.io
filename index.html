Who can join?

Anybody! Whether you're an artist, musician, writer, or engineer, we want you in our community. We believe everyone has a creative vision to share.

What do we do?

We connect people from different backgrounds to build creative projects using technology. We'll have meetings every week where you can hang out with people with similar interests, talk about your projects, create teams to epxlore your ideas, and learn cool things from each other.

When / where are our meetings?

Sundays at 4pm. The room will usually be on Mudd 6th floor, but it is subject to change. Check our Facebook page to stay up-to-date with the latest meeting info.

Cool!. Can I join your mailing list?

Sure! Click the "join" link on the bottom-left.

How else can I contact you?

Email al3856@columbia.edu or sm4788@columbia.edu.
