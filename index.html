Who can join?

Anybody! Whether you're an artist, musician, writer,or engineer, we want you in our community. We believe everyone has a creative vision to share.

We connect people from different backgroudns to build creative projects using technology. We'll have meetings every week where you can hang out with people with similar interests, talk about your projects, create teams to epxlore your ideas, and learn cool things from each other.
